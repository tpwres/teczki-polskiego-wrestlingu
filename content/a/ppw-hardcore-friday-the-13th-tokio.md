PpW Hardcore Friday the 13th 東京 (_Tokio_) is an upcoming show by [PpW Ewenement Wrestling](@/o/ppw.md), set to take place on 13.03.2026 in Tokio, in a venue called ["Tokyo Square in Itabashi"][are-wa-tokyo-square-desu]. The show will be a cooperation with [Big Japan Pro Wrestling][bjw] and other local promotions.

<!-- more -->

On 6.12.2025 Shigehiro Irie and Akane Fujita appeared at [Duch Świąt, Smak Krwi](@/e/ppw/2025-12-06-ppw-duch-swiat-smak-krwi.md). Although PpW made no official statement, it is a possibility that the deal came to be through the two guests who'd worked for multiple Japanese federations, including in the Tokyo Square.
