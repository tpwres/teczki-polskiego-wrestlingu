Złote Ewenementy (_The Golden [Ewenements](@/o/ppw.md#name)_) are a [PpW Ewenement Wrestling](@/o/ppw.md) awards in several categories, voted on by fans.

<!-- more -->

## Editions

### Złote Ewenementy 2025

The first edition took place in December 2025, with fans casting votes via a Google Docs form.

#### Categories awarded

- Wrestler Roku 2025 (_Wrestler of the Year_)
- Walka Roku 2025 (_Match of the Year_)
- Moment Roku 2025 (_Moment of the Year_)
- Debiutant Roku 2025 (_Newcomer of the Year_)
- Najlepszy Plakat Gali 2025 (_Best Show Poster_)
