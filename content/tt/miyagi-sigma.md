Miyagi Sigma are a Polish tag team consisting of [Miyagi Shida](@/w/miyagi-shida.md) & [Sigma Boy xD](@/w/sigma-boy.md).

## History

### Prime Time Wrestling (2025-)

[Miyagi Shida](@/w/miyagi-shida.md) & [Sigma Boy xD](@/w/sigma-boy.md) first teamed up at [PTW: All About the Moné](@/e/ptw/2025-07-27-ptw-all-about-the-mone.md) to take on [Royal Rebels](@/tt/royal-rebels.md) ([Alex Brave](@/w/alex-brave.md) & [Vincent Caravaggio](@/w/vincent-caravaggio.md)). Despite initially losing, they persevered in their attempts as a tag team, until eventually winning the [PTW Tag Team Championship](@/c/ptw-tag-team-championship.md) at [PTW: Krew, Pot i Łzy](@/e/ptw/2026-05-23-ptw-krew-pot-i-lzy.md).
