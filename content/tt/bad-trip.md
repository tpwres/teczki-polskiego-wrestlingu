Bad Trip are a Polish tag team consisting of [Gustav Gryffin](@/w/gustav-gryffin.md) & [Goblin](@/w/goblin.md).

## History

### PpW Ewenement Pro Wrestling (2025-)

Initially bitter enemies, Gustav and Goblin were pushed by circumstances to for an uneasy alliance. At [Mistrzowskie Rozdanie 2](@/e/ppw/2025-09-20-ppw-mistrzowskie-rozdanie-2.md) Gustav - then a member of [Zmowa](@/tt/zmowa.md) - turned on the stable leader [Mister Z](@/w/mister-z.md), turning face in the process. Later at [Wjazd Na Rewir](@/e/ppw/2025-10-24-ppw-wjazd-na-rewir.md) Gryffin severed the remaining ties to the group by helping [Goblin](@/w/goblin.md) get the upper hand over Mister Z.

Goblin then suffered a crushing defeat at the hands of another Zmowa member, [Olgierd](@/w/olgierd.md), at [Duch Świąt, Smak Krwi](@/e/ppw/2025-12-06-ppw-duch-swiat-smak-krwi.md), losing his [PpW Championship](@/c/ppw-championship.md) in the process. At the [following show](@/e/ppw/2026-01-16-ppw-najlepsza-gala-w-miescie-hardcore-edition.md) the two former PpW Champions reluctantly teamed up to help each other exact revenge on Zmowa. A month later at [Dwa Na Dwa](@/e/ppw/2026-02-28-ppw-dwa-na-dwa.md) the duo took on a proper tag team name: Bad Trip. Eventually their cooperation would pay off, as they snatched the [PpW Tag Team Championship](@/c/ppw-tag-team-championship.md) from Zmowa at [Ledwo Legalne 6](@/e/ppw/2026-05-16-ppw-ledwo-legalne-6.md).
