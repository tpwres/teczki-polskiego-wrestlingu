Biuro Prasowe (_Press Office_) is a Polish villainous stable created by, and mostly active in, [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md).

## History

### Legacy of Wrestling (2025-)

In a lead-up to [Legacy of Wrestling Vol.2](@/e/low/2025-04-06-low-2.md), Legacy shared a [voice message][glosowa] from [Arek Paterek](@/w/arek-paterek.md). He claimed to fear for his safety in Legacy's current regime and requested the addition of an "inviolability clause" to his contract with the promotion.

At the show proper, in addition to providing commentary, he introduced Legacy's new acting chairman, [Wiktor Longman](@/w/wiktor-longman.md), and also accompanied [Erik Šlotíř](@/w/erik-slotir.md) during his match, posing as a seemingly villainous manager. After the show, Paterek announced the creation of a new stable called ["Biuro Prasowe"](@/tt/biuro-prasowe.md), with Erik becoming his first "client". In parallel, Paterek started his attempts to recruit [Sambor](@/w/sambor.md).
