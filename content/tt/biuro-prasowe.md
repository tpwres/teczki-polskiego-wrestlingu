Biuro Prasowe (_Press Office_), also known as Biuro Prasowe Arka Paterka (_Arek Paterek's Press Office_) is a Polish villainous stable created by, and mostly active in, [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md). Biuro is led by its Spokesman, [Arek Paterek](@/w/arek-paterek.md).

## History

### Legacy of Wrestling (2025-)

#### Formation and initial members (2025)

In a lead-up to [Legacy of Wrestling Vol.2](@/e/low/2025-04-06-low-2.md), Legacy shared a [voice message][glosowa] from [Arek Paterek](@/w/arek-paterek.md). He claimed to fear for his safety in Legacy's current regime and requested the addition of an "inviolability clause" to his contract with the promotion.

At the show proper, in addition to providing commentary, he introduced Legacy's new acting chairman, [Wiktor D.](@/w/wiktor-longman.md), and also accompanied [Erik Šlotíř](@/w/erik-slotir.md) during his match, posing as a seemingly villainous manager.
After the show, Paterek [introduced][otwarcie] his new faction, with Erik becoming his first "client".
In parallel, Paterek started his attempts to recruit [Sambor](@/w/sambor.md).

At [Legacy vol. 3](@/e/low/2025-07-11-low-3.md), Paterek orchestrated a title match for Šlotíř, who faced Bahraini wrestler Yaqoob and the [MZW Champion](@/c/mzw-championship.md) [Matt Buckna](@/w/matt-buckna.md) in an impromptu Triple Threat Match, won by the defending Champion.
At the end of the show, Paterek was seen talking to Sambor, telling him he'd surrounded himself with the wrong people.
He then added that it could be very easily remedied, implying he wanted Sambor to join him and Erik Šlotíř in the growing faction.

Šlotíř was scheduled to represent Biuro in a 12 Weapons of Christmas Match against [Disco Pablo](@/w/disco-pablo.md) at [Legacy of Wrestling: Four the Glory](@/e/low/2025-12-20-low-4.md), however was forced to cancel due to an injury.
The match still happened, with a surprise debut of MZW's [Tony Sheen](@/w/riverman.md) taking Erik's place.
After the match, Sheen opened the twelfth, final gift, revealing it to be Biuro's T-shirt. He put it on to confirm he had joined the stable.

#### Expansion (2026-)

Afterwards, in the lead-up to [Legacy of Wrestling: Open Season](@/e/low/2026-05-24-low-5.md), Biuro [posted][papiery] yet-unsigned release papers for Erik Šlotíř.
At the show itself, Paterek fired Erik from Biuro Prasowe _in absentia_, while Tony Sheen was officially inducted as a member.
Following Sheen's loss to [Marcelito](@/w/marcelito.md) (after a distraction by [Disco Pablo](@/w/disco-pablo.md)), the angry Paterek [promised revenge][arek-sie-wkurzyl], invoking a biblical quote from Ezekiel 25:17 (in fact quoting [Samuel L. Jackson's monologue][25-17] from _Pulp Fiction_).
He challenged them both to a six-man tag team match against Biuro Prasowe, at Legacy's upcoming [Enter the Dragon](@/e/low/2026-07-10-low-6.md) show.
Confronted with the fact that Biuro Prasowe still had only one wrestler on payroll, he presented his newest acquisition: [KPW's](@/o/kpw.md) [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md).

With the math still not working out for a six-man match, Paterek toured Poland to recruit the third member of his team.
On 3.06.2026 Biuro posted a [video][paskal-nie-prasuje] of his attempt to recruit [Markus Paskal](@/w/markus-paskal.md), a plan which backfired rather badly.
During the search, he also visited a nondescript warehouse, stepping into one of the reunion training sessions from the former [DFW](@/o/dfw.md) crew, however none of the trainees here was to Paterek's particular liking.

Just before the show, the odds for 6-man match were firmly favoring the babyface's team, as [Bartosz Plata](@/w/plata.md), mostly known from [PpW](@/o/ppw.md) was booked to join Pablo and Marcelito.
However at the show itself, the situation flipped: Plata, after making his entrance with the other babyfaces, unzipped his signature track sweatshirt, revealed Biuro's colors underneath and attacked Marcelito from behind.
Sambor, fed up with Biuro harassing him for over a year now, joined the fray as a 6th member of the match, evening the odds. After a hard-fought battle, Biuro managed to score its first win as a group.

### Maniac Zone Wrestling (2025-)

Due to MZW and Legacy's tight cooperation, Biuro's storyline usually extends to MZW shows. For his [Maniac Zone Wrestling](@/o/mzw.md) debut at [Green Madness](@/e/mzw/2025-06-28-mzw-green-madness.md), where he lost to [Goblin](@/w/goblin.md), Erik donned Biuro's colors all throughout the match.

At [No Time To Die 2](@/e/mzw/2026-03-28-mzw-no-time-to-die-2.md), "Riverman" Tony Sheen was accompanied by Paterek, serving as his manager. Riverman continued to handle Biuro's business in MZW and was supposed to recruit Sambor at the [next show](@/e/mzw/2026-06-20-mzw-forever-2.md), however this did not come into fruition.
