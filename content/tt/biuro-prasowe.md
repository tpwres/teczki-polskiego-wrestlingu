Biuro Prasowe (_Press Office_) is a Polish villainous stable created by, and mostly active in, [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md).

## History

### Legacy of Wrestling (2025-)

The stable was created by [Arek Paterek](@/w/arek-paterek.md) following [Legacy of Wrestling vol. 2](@/e/low/2025-04-06-low-2.md), with the first member being [Erik Šlotíř](@/w/erik-slotir.md).
