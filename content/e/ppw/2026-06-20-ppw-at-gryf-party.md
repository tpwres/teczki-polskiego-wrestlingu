GRYF PARTY is an annual gathering and event held by GRYF MC, a motorcycle club with chapters in Siedlce and other cities in Poland. In 2026, the 24th Gryf Party was held in Serpelice, a village located on the Bug river close to the Polish-Belarussian border, over the weekend of June 19-21. Among the various attractions planned for Saturday was a wrestling showcase by [PpW](@/o/ppw.md).

## Reveal

PpW and Gryf's Radom Chapter posted an [Instagram reel][biesiad-reel] on June 16, 2026, just a couple of days before the event. However, based on the Facebook event page, the schedule was posted one week earlier, on June 10th. In the reel, [Biesiad](@/w/biesiad.md) and [Jacob Crane](@/w/jacob-crane.md) - speaking Polish and English respectively - invite viewers to the gathering. Biesiad also enthusiastically promises some tables, ladders and chairs action. Intercut are clips from recent PpW events, including the Chain God Realm Match from [Duch Świąt, Smak Krwi](@/e/ppw/2025-12-06-ppw-duch-swiat-smak-krwi.md) and the final moment of [Goblin](@/w/goblin.md) vs [Gustav](@/w/gustav-gryffin.md) III from [Ledwo Legalne 5](@/e/ppw/2025-06-07-ppw-ledwo-legalne-5.md).
