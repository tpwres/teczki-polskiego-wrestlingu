Inicjatywa Wrestling (_Wrestling Initiative_) is an upcoming show by [PpW Ewenement's](@/o/ppw.md). It is set to take place in the {{ map_pin(v="Rozbrat") }}[Rozbrat][rozbrat] complex in Poznań, an alternative culture and squatting centre. It will be the first proper PpW event in the city - although PpW already held showcases at the [Poznań International Fair](@/v/targi-poznan.md) beer festival, they were non-canon to PpW's product, serving as a place for experimentation with new gimmicks and personas.
