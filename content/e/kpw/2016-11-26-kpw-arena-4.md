Nowy Rozdział (_The New Chapter_) was held in Gdańsk, in the gymnastics hall of {% map_pin(v="Hallera 16") %}CKZiU 1{% end %}, a complex of various educational institutions at the high school or vocational school level. Much smaller in scope than the previous event, it had only one foreign guest, British wrestler [Elliott Jordan](@/w/elliott-jordan.md).
