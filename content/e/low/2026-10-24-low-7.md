What's in the Box? is an upcoming show by [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md), and the second Legacy event in Kraków in 2026. This time it will be held in {{ map_pin(v="Klub Kwadrat") }}Klub Kwadrat. The name of the show is a quote from David Fincher's film [Seven][dobry-film-ale-przewidywalna-koncowka]. It was announced three on 7.07.2026, three days before the [sixth Legacy event](@/e/low/2026-07-10-low-6.md).

## Build-up

* At the [last show](@/e/low/2026-07-10-low-6.md) [Max Speed](@/w/max-speed.md) challenged [Jakub](@/w/jakub.md) for the [Legacy of Wrestling European Championship](@/c/low-european-championship.md). Jakub initially declined, but ended up agreeing after all, intending to humiliate Speed.
