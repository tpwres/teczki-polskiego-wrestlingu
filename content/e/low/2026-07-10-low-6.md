Enter the Dragon was a show by [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md), and the second Legacy event to be held in the [Tauron Arena](@/v/tauron-arena.md) in Kraków, accompanying the manga-themed fan convention Ryucon.

## Build-up

* While the name of the show was made public on 24.03.2026 via an image on Legacy's Facebook, the date of the event was first found by fans on Ryucon's website prior to any announcements by Legacy.
* On 20.05.2026 [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md) announced his involvement in a [Facebook post][tournee-lesaka] with upcoming dates of his appearances.
* At the [previous Legacy show](@/e/low/2026-05-24-low-5.md), [Max Speed](@/w/max-speed.md) challenged [Wiktor Longman](@/w/wiktor-longman.md) to a fight at Ryucon, which he accepted.
* At the same show, [Syriusz Dziedzic](@/w/dziedzic.md) challenged [Sinister](@/w/sinister.md) to a match at Ryucon under the condition that if he wins, Dziedzic will once again join him. Dziedzic countered with a stipulation of his own: if he wins, Sinister must quit Legacy forever.
* The show's poster published on 26.05.2026 featured English wrestler Trent Seven, announcing his participation in the show. Seven was previously seen at [PTW #3](@/e/ptw/2022-11-26-ptw-3-legends.md), where he fought [Joe Hendry](@/w/joe-hendry.md) for the IMPACT Digital Media Championship.
* Following [Tony Sheen's](@/w/riverman.md) loss to [Marcelito](@/w/marcelito.md) (after a distraction by [Disco Pablo](@/w/disco-pablo.md)), angry [Arek Paterek](@/w/arek-paterek.md) [promised revenge][arek-sie-wkurzyl], invoking a biblical quote from Ezekiel 25:17 (in fact quoting [Samuel L. Jackson's monologue][25-17] from _Pulp Fiction_). He challenged them both to a six-man tag team match against Biuro Prasowe. Confronted with the fact that Biuro Prasowe consists of only one wrestler, he presented his newest acquisition: [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md).
* With the math still not working out for a six-man match, Paterek toured Poland to recruit the third member of his team. On 3.06.2026 Biuro posted a [video][paskal-nie-prasuje] of his attempt to recruit [Markus Paskal](@/w/markus-paskal.md), which backfired.
* On 14.06.2026 Paterek posted a [video of him visiting a nondescript industrial building][paterek-w-garazu], where he discovers a [DFW](@/o/dfw.md) training session, after getting a tip-off about some "young guns" (_młode wilki_, lit. "young wolves") available for recruitment. [PJ Blake](@/w/pj-blake.md), acting as the leader, briefly presented three of his trainees, but Paterek did not see any of them as fit to represent Biuro Prasowe. (The term "young wolves" refers to a [Polish film of the same name][mlodzi-wilcy], whereas Blake's lines are a nod to a scene from another film, [Kiler][kiler], parodying Young Wolves.)
* The match between [Syriusz Dziedzic](@/w/dziedzic.md) and [Sinister](@/w/sinister.md) was officially confirmed on 20.06.2026, with the added Falls Count Anywhere stipulation.
* The match between Max Speed and Wiktor Longman was officially confirmed on 2.07.2026.
* On 4.07.2026 Legacy announced a match between the former [KPW Champion](@/c/kpw-championship.md) [Zefir](@/w/zefir.md) and the [ex-PpW Champion](@/c/ppw-championship.md) [Olgierd](@/w/olgierd.md). This will be Olgierd's second fight with a KPW wrestler (following his bout with [Filip Fux](@/w/filip-fux.md) at [KPW Arena 34](@/e/kpw/2026-06-26-kpw-arena-34.md)) as well as Zefir's second clash with a non-KPW worker (the first being against [Boro](@/w/boro.md) at the [previous Legacy show](@/e/low/2026-05-24-low-5.md)).
* On 5.07.2026 Legacy unveiled the main event of the show: Trent Seven vs [Jakub](@/w/jakub.md) for the [Legacy of Wrestling European Championship](@/c/low-european-championship.md).
* On 8.07.2026 Legacy announced the first of six contenders to take part in a Gauntlet Match for a championship contract: the Italian wrestler [Sam Della Valle](@/w/sam-rogers.md).
* The day before the show, a Six-Man Tag Team Match between  between Biuro Prasowe and Team [Disco Pablo](@/w/disco-pablo.md) was finally confirmed. While babyfaces enlisted the help of [Bartosz Plata](@/w/plata.md), Biuro Prasowe remained one man short.

### Recap

* [Olgierd](@/w/olgierd.md) didn't appear at the show due to an injury. His spot on the card was filled in by "a real dragon" - [Jacob Crane](@/w/jacob-crane.md).
* Crane showed off his Japanese skills by speaking the language to the audience a few times.
* [Syriusz Dziedzic](@/w/dziedzic.md) and [Sinister](@/w/sinister.md) largely brawled outside the ring, keeping with the stipulation of the match.
* At one point, when fighting at the entrance ramp, Syriusz pinned Sinister who kicked out at two - only for his music to hit as if he'd won. [Sędzia Michał](@/w/sedzia-michal.md) had to rush over to the person in charge of the music to have him cut it.
* For the announced Six-Man Tag Team Match, Biuro Prasowe came out missing a person - only [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md) and [Tony Sheen](@/w/riverman.md) were present, accompanied by [Arek Paterek](@/w/arek-paterek.md). After the babyfaces pointed out that the Biuro is one man short, Paterek answered that they always have "a full alphabet of plans" and that "it is already three-vs-three". With that said, [Bartosz Plata](@/w/plata.md) unzipped his longsleeve, revealing a Biuro Prasowe T-shirt underneath, and proceeded to attack his former friends which earned him plenty of heat from the audience. After a while, [Sambor](@/w/sambor.md) ran into the ring to aid Pablo and Marcelito. 
* After losing the match Sambor attacked Pablo, blaming him for his string of losses.
* Next, [Adrian Zgórski](@/w/adrian-zgorski.md) addressed the audience, praising them for their continuing support of Legacy, [KPW](@/o/kpw.md), [MZW](@/o/mzw.md) and [PpW](@/o/ppw.md), and invited a guest to the ring: Patrykos from the WrestleOne YouTube channel, known to the audience from the [previous show](@/e/low/2026-05-24-low-5.md). The fans were initially vocally against Patrykos, but when he was soon confronted by [Oskar Haller](@/w/oskar-haller.md), the heat moved to him instead.
* Not a long time later [Marco Hammers](@/w/marco-hammers.md) made his way into the ring and attacked Haller, seemingly aligning himself with Patrykos. The two then proceeded to drink a couple of beers, and threw a few more into the crowd.
* After the segment a group of Star Wars cosplayers had a flashy lightsaber fight, accompanied by John Williams's [Duel of the Fates][fata-sie-bija] from [Star Wars Episode I: The Phantom Menace][meesa-jar-jar-binks]. The duel was followed by an extended stretch of nothing happening, prompting the fans to chant "Where's the show?" It wasn't until Adrian Zgórski entered the ring and took the microphone that it became clear that it had been a break in the show - a fact that many of the fans had missed.
* Despite being called a Gauntlet Match, the rules were that of a Royal Rumble: the bout began with two wrestlers in the ring (Della Valle and Boro), with new contestants entering at set intervals.
* Boro came out holding a bouquet of red roses, which he distributed among female fans. The final rose he gave to his opponent, who accepted the flower only to destroy it shortly afterwards.
* Despite being called a Gauntlet Match, the rules were that of a Royal Rumble: the bout began with two wrestlers in the ring (Della Valle and Boro), with new contestants entering at set intervals.
* [Chemik](@/w/chemik.md) entered a brief alliance with [Sam Della Valle](@/w/sam-rogers.md) against [Filip Fux](@/w/filip-fux.md) ("You! Me! Friends!"), only to backstab him seconds later.
* Order of eliminations in the Gauntlet Match:
  1. Chemik eliminated Sam Della Valle
  2. Oskar Haller eliminated Markus Paskal
  3. Filip Fux eliminated Oskar Haller
  4. Boro eliminated Filip Fux
  5. Chemik eliminated Boro
* After an arduous fight, [Max Speed](@/w/max-speed.md) managed to defeat [Wiktor Longman](@/w/wiktor-longman.md). Wiktor praised Speed for finally proving his worth by overcoming him, alluding to Speed's debut match against Longman at [PTW Underground 16](@/e/ptw/2023-07-30-ptw-underground-16.md) where Max lost by DQ.
* Longman then announced that it had been his final fight as a wrestler - he'd still remain active in the role of Acting Chairman, but "the mean Wiktor Longman was no more". His promo earned him a prolonged standing ovation, and even Max Speed was visibly touched, shedding a tear or five.
* During the main event, [Sędzia Seweryn](@/w/sedzia-seweryn.md) was knocked out when Trent Seven lifted [Jakub](@/w/jakub.md), resulting in Trent's pin not being counted.
* Following Jakub's victory, Speed came out demanding a Championship fight. Jakub declined, belittling Speed and making fun of his perceived obedience. In the end, however, Jakub agreed to fight Speed for the title at [the next show](@/e/low/2026-10-24-low-7.md), if only to humiliate him. Speed, incensed by Jakub's mocking that he has no bite, pounced at the Champion and posed with his title belt for a few moments, before leaving the Championship over his chest and walking away.
* Present at the show, sitting next to the commentary table, was [Karyna](@/w/karyna.md).
