Enter the Dragon is an upcoming show by [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md), and the second Legacy event to be held in the [Tauron Arena](@/v/tauron-arena.md) in Kraków, accompanying the manga-themed fan convention Ryucon.

## Build-up

* While the name of the show was made public on 24.03.2026 via an image on Legacy's Facebook, the date of the event was first found by fans on Ryucon's website prior to any announcements by Legacy.
* On 20.05.2026 [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md) announced his involvement in a [Facebook post][tournee-lesaka] with upcoming dates of his appearances.
* At the [previous Legacy show](@/e/low/2026-05-24-low-5.md), [Max Speed](@/w/max-speed.md) challenged [Wiktor Longman](@/w/wiktor-longman.md) to a fight at Ryucon, which he accepted.
* At the same show, [Syriusz Dziedzic](@/w/dziedzic.md) challenged [Sinister](@/w/sinister.md) to a match at under the condition that if he wins, Dziedzic will once again join him. Dziedzic countered with a stipulation of his own: if he wins, Sinister must quit Legacy forever.
* The show's poster published on 26.05.2026 featured English wrestler Trent Seven, announcing his participation in the show. Seven was previously seen at [PTW #3](@/e/ptw/2022-11-26-ptw-3-legends.md), where he fought [Joe Hendry](@/w/joe-hendry.md) for the IMPACT Digital Media Championship.
* After [Tony Sheen](@/w/tony-sheen.md)'s loss to [Marcelito](@/w/marcelito.md)(after distraction from [Disco Pablo](@/w/disco-pablo.md)), angry [Arek Paterek](@/w/arek-paterek.md) [promised revenge](https://www.instagram.com/p/DZErz22ovd9/), invoking biblical quote from _Ezekiel 25;17_ (under a wrong wording by Samuel L Jackson's monologue in _Pulp Fiction_). He challenged them both to a 6-man tag team match against Biuro Prasowe. Confronted with the fact that Biuro Prasowe consists of only one wrestler, he presents their newest acquisition: [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md)
* With math still not enough for a 6-man match, Paterek toured Poland to recruit the third member of his team. On 3.06, Biuro posted a [video](https://www.instagram.com/p/DZHfkXOM-Tx/) of his attempt to recruit [Markus Paskal](@/w/markus-paskal.md), which backfired.
* On 14.06, Paterek posted a video of him visiting the [DFW](@/o/dfw.md) training, after getting a tip-off about some _young guns_ (originally: _młode wilki_) being available for recruitment. PJ Blake, acting as a leader, briefly presented 3 of his _trainees_, but Paterek did not see any of them as fit to represent Biuro Prasowe.
