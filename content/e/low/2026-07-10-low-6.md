Enter the Dragon is an upcoming show by [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md), and the second Legacy event to be held in the [Tauron Arena](@/v/tauron-arena.md) in Kraków, accompanying the manga-themed fan convention Ryucon.

## Build-up

* While the name of the show was made public on 24.03.2026 via an image on Legacy's Facebook, the date of the event was first found by fans on Ryucon's website prior to any announcements by Legacy.
* On 20.05.2026 [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md) announced his involvement in a [Facebook post][tournee-lesaka] with upcoming dates of his appearances.
* At the [previous Legacy show](@/e/low/2026-05-24-low-5.md), [Max Speed](@/w/max-speed.md) challenged [Wiktor Longman](@/w/wiktor-longman.md) to a fight at Ryucon, which he accepted.
* At the same show, [Syriusz Dziedzic](@/w/dziedzic.md) challenged [Sinister](@/w/sinister.md) to a match at Ryucon under the condition that if he wins, Dziedzic will once again join him. Dziedzic countered with a stipulation of his own: if he wins, Sinister must quit Legacy forever.
* The show's poster published on 26.05.2026 featured English wrestler Trent Seven, announcing his participation in the show. Seven was previously seen at [PTW #3](@/e/ptw/2022-11-26-ptw-3-legends.md), where he fought [Joe Hendry](@/w/joe-hendry.md) for the IMPACT Digital Media Championship.
* Following [Tony Sheen's](@/w/tony-sheen.md) loss to [Marcelito](@/w/marcelito.md) (after a distraction by [Disco Pablo](@/w/disco-pablo.md)), angry [Arek Paterek](@/w/arek-paterek.md) [promised revenge][arek-sie-wkurzyl] invoking a biblical quote from Ezekiel 25:17 (in fact quoting [Samuel L. Jackson's monologue][25-17] from _Pulp Fiction_). He challenged them both to a six-man tag team match against Biuro Prasowe. Confronted with the fact that Biuro Prasowe consists of only one wrestler, he presented his newest acquisition: [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md).
* With the math still not working out for a six-man match, Paterek toured Poland to recruit the third member of his team. On 3.06.2026 Biuro posted a [video][paskal-nie-prasuje] of his attempt to recruit [Markus Paskal](@/w/markus-paskal.md), which backfired.
* On 14.06.2026 Paterek posted a video of him visiting a [DFW](@/o/dfw.md) training, after getting a tip-off about some "young guns" (_młode wilki_, lit. "young wolves") being available for recruitment. [PJ Blake](@/w/pj-blake.md), acting as the leader, briefly presented thee of his trainees, but Paterek did not see any of them as fit to represent Biuro Prasowe. (The term "young wolves" refers to a [Polish film of the same name][mlodzi-wilcy], whereas Blake's lines are a nod to a scene from [Kiler][kiler] parodying Young Wolves.)
