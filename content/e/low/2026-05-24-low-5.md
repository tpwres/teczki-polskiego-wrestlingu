Open Season is an upcoming show by [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md), to be held in the usual [Królestwo music club](@/v/krolestwo-katowice.md) in Katowice. The show's name and poster seem to be a reference to a phrase popular in Internet fandom since [Jakub](@/w/jakub.md)'s heel turn: _Sezon Na Lisa_ (_Fox-hunting season_), showing fandom's animosity toward former hero.
