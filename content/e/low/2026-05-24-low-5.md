Open Season is an upcoming show by [Legacy of Wrestling](@/o/low.md), to be held in the usual [Królestwo music club](@/v/krolestwo-katowice.md) in Katowice. The event's name and poster seem to be a reference to a phrase popular online among the fans since [Jakub's](@/w/jakub.md) heel turn: _sezon na lisa_ (_fox-hunting season_), showing the fandom's animosity toward former hero.

## Build-up

* The first match, announced on 10.04.2026, was [Chemik](@/w/chemik.md) vs [Max Speed](@/w/max-speed.md).
* The second match to be unveiled, on 28.04.2026, was the main event fight between [Jakub](@/w/jakub.md) and [Filip Fux](@/w/filip-fux.md) for the [Legacy of Wrestling European Championship](@/c/low-european-championship.md).
* On 7.05.2026 came the announcement of [Syriusz Dziedzic](@/w/dziedzic.md) vs [KPW's](@/o/kpw.md) [Eryk Lesak](@/w/eryk-lesak.md), in his debut for Legacy.
* On 11.05.2026 Legacy announced another debut: [Zefir](@/w/zefir.md), in a match against [Boro](@/w/boro.md).
* According to information published on 13.05.2026, Patrykos from WrestleOne YouTube channel will attend the show and record a report.
* On 15.05.2026 broke the apparent streak of KPW vs non-KPW match-ups by posting [Tony Sheen](@/w/riverman.md) vs [Marcelito](@/w/marcelito.md).
* On 17.05.2026 [Marco Hammers](@/w/marco-hammers.md) published a [video][marco-na-zakupach] in which he issued an Open Challenge, seemingly for the [MZW Championship](@/c/mzw-championship.md). The video itself parodies [Matt Buckna's](@/w/matt-buckna.md) [promo][matta-nie-bylo] from [MZW No Time to Die](@/e/mzw/2026-03-28-mzw-no-time-to-die-2.md).
* On 18.05.2026 Legacy posted that a Four Way Elimination Match would take place at the show, the contenders being [Olgierd](@/w/olgierd.md), [Disco Pablo](@/w/disco-pablo.md), [Sambor](@/w/sambor.md) and Italian wrestler Omar Prince, making his Polish debut.
* Marco's Open Challenge was [answered][oskar-sie-wprasza] on 19.05.2026 by [Oskar Haller](@/w/oskar-haller.md). However, [Marco's promo][jednak-nie-o-pas] from 21.05 clarified that the match won't be for the MZW Championship - although a title bout (for an unspecified belt) could be possible at a later date, if Oskar does well. The the match was officially confirmed on 22.05, with the [post][markoskar] reiterating that it'll be a non-title match.
