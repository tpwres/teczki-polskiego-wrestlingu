"Ślepa Furia" (_Blind Fury_) was a show by [Prime Time Wrestling](@/o/ptw.md).

## Build-up

* The first person, announced on 8.07.2026, to appear at the show was ["Starboy" Nano Lopez](@/w/nano-lopez.md), albeit without a match-up.
* The first match, announced later the same day, was a fight for the [PTW Championship](@/c/ptw-championship.md) between the reigning Champion [Spartan](@/w/spartan.md), and the English challenger Jakk Selström.
* On 9.07.2026 PTW announced a match between [Nic Wesker](@/w/nic-wesker.md) and [Daniel Razor](@/w/daniel-razor.md).
* Later the same day came the announcement of [CJ Klakson](@/w/cj-klakson.md) vs Stan the Slav.
* On 10.07.2026 PTW announced [Vincent Caravaggio](@/w/vincent-caravaggio.md) (forced out of holiday by [Łukasz Okoński](@/w/lukasz-okonski.md)) vs [Polski Niedźwiedź](@/w/polski-niedzwiedz.md) (returning after an injury, last seen at [Krew, Pot i Łzy](@/e/ptw/2026-05-23-ptw-krew-pot-i-lzy.md)).
* On 11.07.2026 the promotion informed that the opening match of the show is going to be [Fabian](@/w/fabian.md) vs [Boguś](@/w/bogus.md).
* The next match was [Alex Brave](@/w/alex-brave.md) vs [Flex Skywalker](@/w/flex-skywalker.md), revealed on 13.07.2026.
* Later the same day PTW announced a Tag Team Match between Proroctwo ([Whiteblade](@/w/whiteblade.md) & [Sawicki](@/w/sawicki.md)) and [El G](@/w/el-g.md) & El Clavo.
* The next day PTW announced [Misiek](@/w/misiek.md) vs Stone Malone from Scotland.
* On 15.07.2026 PTW announced that [Łukasz Okoński](@/w/lukasz-okonski.md) will open the show.
* On 17.07.2026 the federation announced the crew of host/ring announcer [Krzysztof Skwarczyński](@/w/krzysztof-skwarczynski.md), referee [Klaudiusz](@/w/sedzia-klaudiusz.md) and commentator [Adam Goldschmidt](@/w/adam-goldschmidt.md).
* It was followed a few hours later by the announcement of [Sawicki's](@/w/sawicki.md) confession. Sawicki was last seen at [Krew, Pot i Łzy](@/e/ptw/2026-05-23-ptw-krew-pot-i-lzy.md) where he joined [Whiteblade](@/w/whiteblade.md) and [Damian Lambert's](@/w/damian-lambert.md) group, Proroctwo.

### Highlights

* [Krzysztof Skwarczyński](@/w/krzysztof-skwarczynski.md) opened the show as usual, but due to taking over commentary in [Resivny](@/w/resivny.md)'s absence, he could not host the show as usual. Thus, [Łukasz "Prezes" Okoński](@/w/lukasz-okonski.md) presented a replacement: [Arkadiusz "Pan" Pawłowski"](@/w/pan-pawlowski.md), returning to the role for the first time since [PTW Underground 25: Wrestlingowe Mikołajki](@/e/ptw/2024-12-07-ptw-underground-25.md).
* After his loss, [Fabian](@/w/fabian.md) blamed it on VaDique, who "did not properly support him". VaDique in turn claimed nobody fires her, so she fired Fabian back.
* For his match against [Polski Niedźwiedź](@/w/polski-niedzwiedz.md), [Vincent Caravaggio](@/w/vincent-caravaggio.md) entered in vacation clothes, complete with a suitcase. During the match, a time-limit of 6 minutes was mentioned, as Caravaggio was in a rush to get the plane for his vacation. Though Caravaggio won, due to many obstructions before and during the match, he did not make it in time and left frustrated.
* [Kalom](@/w/kalom.md), formerly the [MCW Champion](@/c/mcw-championship.md), made his PTW debut as [Daniel Razor](@/w/daniel-razor.md)'s _Lucky 8_ contest mentee. After Razor's match, prone Kalom appeared on stage, followed by [Fabian](@/w/fabian.md) kicking him around. Razor came in to check on him, only to be laid out too.
* [PTW Underground Championship](@/c/ptw-underground-championship.md) Open Challenge was set-up for champions only. Thus, it became a Champion vs Champion vs Champion vs Champion match, as ["Starboy" Nano Lopez](@/w/nano-lopez.md) successfully defended against both [PTW Tag Team Champions](@/c/ptw-tag-team-championship.md), as well as XWP Champion [Blaze](@/w/blaze.md).
* Before the break, commentators proceeded to talk on-screen about the events on the show so far. Throughout the show, [Adam Goldschmidt](@/w/adam-goldschmidt.md) was comically overselling [Herno](@/w/sedzia-herno.md)'s attack on him from _[Godzina z Goldschmidtem][godzina]_ YouTube show, even wearing a neckbrace. After getting into contact with Arkadiusz Pawłowski, starstruck Goldschmidt declared a miracle has happened due to Pawłowski's "blessing" and ditched the neckbrace for rest of the show.
* As Proroctwo made their entrance for an announced Tag Team Match, [Sawicki](@/w/sawicki.md) debuted a new persona, wearing white fur robe, paint covering half of his face and a new music (Rammstein's _Engel_). He took the mic and explained his attack on Synowie Lasu, firstly seeming regretful and requesting penance. He interviewed random crowd member to give him penance, but before she could speak, he took the mic away and declared that the attack was earned so no penance is needed. Then, Sawicki proceeded to tell a tale of his career, painting himself as _little white bear_. The story ended with his loss against _big, stinky grizzly bear_ (Misiek) and then the brutal reality set in. As his weak Sawicki persona is over, he will soon present a "new, stronger and more terrifying" name for himself. He declares that he will stop speaking forever, letting the actions speak for him. Sawicki mentioned that [Prorok Lambert](@/w/damian-lambert.md) was a false prophet, but the prophecy is intact and the end of the world is still coming.
* For a tag team match, El G presented a luchador persona of _El Hijo del G_ to match El Clavo. During the match, he was unmasked, revealing [El G](@/w/el-g.md)'s face to nobydy's surprise.
* After [CJ Klakson](@/w/cj-klakson.md)'s win, Pawłowski proceeded to celebrate Klakson's birthday. Frustrated with the loss, Stan the Slav attacked Klakson and got a birthday cake to his face. Klakson took the mic and declared himself for Gold Rush Rumble 2026, happening at the next show.
* Spartan retained against Jakk Selström.
* In a closing segment, Pawłowski and Okoński presented a big announcement about Krzysztof Skwarczyński. As he will become a father, he will be out of PTW for near future. [Spartan](@/w/spartan.md) entered as well, seemingly to congratulate new father. However, he blamed Okoński for using him as his soldier and later completely ignoring him, to the point of rehiring former villain Pawłowski without any notice. He slapped Okoński, slammed Skwarczyński onto the mat and got his hands on Pawłowski. Members of the roster unsuccesfully entered to stop Spartan's rampage, in following order: [Sigma Boy xD](@/w/sigma-boy.md), [Miyagi Shida](@/w/miyagi-shida.md) and [Sędzia Klaudiusz](@/w/sedzia-klaudiusz.md). Then [Misiek](@/w/misiek.md) entered to face-off with Spartan, who finally left the ring. After brief consultation with the Chairman, Pawłowski announced that Misiek will challenge Spartan for [PTW Championship](@/c/ptw-championship.md) at the very next show.
