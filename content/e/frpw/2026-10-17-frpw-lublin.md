After two rookie showcases, in [May](@/e/frpw/2026-05-03-frpw-dojo-showcase.md) and [July](@/e/frpw/2026-07-19-frpw-dojo-showcase-2.md), FRPW is going to hold a show in Lublin. The event, dubbed "Wrestling w Twoim Mieście" (_Wrestling in Your City_), will take place in the {{ map_pin(v="Centrum Sportu Akademos") }}Akademos Sports Centre. This will be the first FRPW show outside of Warsaw and the first time that wrestling is presented in Lublin.
