Backyardmania IV was a cross-promotion supershow, again hosted by PXW in their {{ map_pin(v="Grunwaldzka 5") }}usual spot in Głuchołazy. The event was planned by the organisers for two days beginning on 3.08.2013, with the first day reserved for training.

No information about the match card survives, and of the only two videos known, just one match and one promo are still available.

### Highlights

* Soon after CMD landed on light tubes, a woman can be seen moving the laundry to another clothesline, farther away from the ring. This is the only known instance of both possible meanings of "clothesline" to be represented in a single video in the history of Polish wrestling.
* Doubt appeared to be legitimately hurt after CMD jumped on him from the middle rope, breaking light tubes on his chest. He stayed down following the spot and apparently broke character, saying "Somebody fucking help me!" (_No kurwa, pomóż mi ktoś!_) in a trembling voice. The match continued, however, with CMD taping another light tube to Doubt's back and once again breaking it with a stomp. Doubt soon recovered from his seeming injury and proceeded to counterattack.
* After the match, Doubt said he'd just defeated the Champion of Poland, implying it was a Championship Match.
* A man - presumably an audience member - approached the ring, pointing at something. Doubt, staying in character, asked him "Are you my new light, and new life? Will you help me fight for good? Will we fuck up this world?!" (_Czy ty jesteś moim nowym światłem, i nowym życiem? Czy ty będziesz pomagał mi walczyć o dobro? Czy będziemy rozkurwiać ten świat?!_). Bemused, the other man replied "No, I won't marry you. Calm down." (_Nie, nie wyjdę za ciebie. Uspokój się._).
* In addition to the regular audience, the show was also watched by some random passers-by, who stopped by the fence.
