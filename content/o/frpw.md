Fundacja Rozwoju Polskiego Wrestlingu (_Foundation for the Development of Polish Wrestling_, FRPW) is a Polish foundation and federation based in Warsaw. The Foundation was established to promote wrestling in Poland and internationally. FRPW organises its own wrestling events, as well as dojo shows of its wrestling school: [Warszawska Szkoła Wrestlingu](@/o/dojo.md). Besides that, it also hosts multiple exhibitions and demo-lessons aimed at youth, and markets its own clothing brand called "Heel Turn".

## History

### Foundation

Fundacja Rozwoju Polskiego Wrestlingu has been formally established in May 2026 by three of the most renowned Polish wrestlers: [Jacob Crane](@/w/jacob-crane.md), [Goblin](@/w/goblin.md) and [Gustav Gryffin](@/w/gustav-gryffin.md). A little earlier, in April, they also founded a clothing brand "Heel Turn", which was created to further promote wrestling merchandise as part of lifestyle and fashion.

FRPW held their first event named [Ewenement Dojo Showcase](@/e/dojo/2026-05-03-dojo_frpw-showcase.md) in May 2026. It was hosted in cooperation with the fellow Warsaw-based federation [PpW](@/o/ppw.md). From that point on FRPW became a fully independent federation with their first solo event being [Dojo Showcase 2](@/e/frpw/2026-07-19-frpw-dojo-showcase-2.md). Despite holding a strong personal connection with PpW - as many of its personel comes from that organisation - Fundacja Rozwoju Polskiego Wrestlingu declares itself to be a fully separate entity with their own events, roster and storylines.

In July 2026 FRPW also officially rebranded Ewenement Dojo - a Warsaw-based wrestling school - to turn it into [Warszawska Szkoła Wrestlingu](@/o/dojo.md). This decision was made to confirm the _de facto_ state of affairs, in which the school was operated mainly by Crane, Gryffin and Goblin, and only symbolically linked to PpW.

## Other ventures

Beside functioning as a federation and a foundation, FRPW also hosts a "Heel Turn" clothing brand focused on promoting wrestling-related merchandise in mainstream fashion. It also cooperates with other Polish wrestlers such as [Gabriel Queen](@/w/gabriel-queen.md).
