Ryucon is an annual, summer anime/manga fan convention, held in Kraków since 2015. The event grew significantly since its initial edition, moving in 2022 to the citys's biggest sports/entertainment facility - [Tauron Arena](@/v/tauron.md), and has been held in there ever since. That year also saw Ryucon invite [PTW](@/o/ptw.md) to present a wrestling show as part of the attractions. The organization returned in 2023 and 2024, but in 2025 and 2026 the organizers chose to host [Legacy](@/o/low.md) instead.
