Pain Killer is a retired Polish backyard wrestler best known for his time in [Dream Factory Wrestling](@/o/dfw.md).

* Years active: 2015-2018

## Professional wrestling career

### Dream Factory Wrestling (2015-2018)

Pain Killer made his debut in [Dream Factory Wrestling](@/o/dfw.md) at their [inaugural show](@/e/dfw/2015-06-20-dfw-showcase.md) in June 2015. He worked alongside [Charlie Grayson](@/w/madman-charlie.md) in a Tag Team Match against [Corin Mear](@/w/corin-mear.md) and [Malmo Boruto](@/w/malmo-boruto.md), but was defeated. He managed to score his first singles win in a match against [Revage](@/w/rafael-kid.md) at [DFW Showcase @ ZST Toru≈Ñ](@/e/dfw/2016-03-10-dfw-zst.md). Later on he was eliminated by [PJ Blake](@/w/pj-blake.md) in a Tournament Quarter Final Match for [DFW Championship](@/c/dfw-championship.md) at [Tournament of Dreams I](@/e/dfw/2016-06-11-dfw-tournament-of-dreams-1.md). His last match took place at the final DFW Show - [Crossroads II](@/e/dfw/2018-06-09-dfw-crosswords-city-2.md), where he was defeated by [Direk](@/w/direk.md). 

After DFW folded, Pain Killer retired from wrestling.
