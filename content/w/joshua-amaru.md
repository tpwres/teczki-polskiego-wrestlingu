Joshua Amaru is a German wrestler, mainly appearing for Berlin-based GWF and Project Nova, as well as in other promotions all over the country, where he won two tag team championships in ERW (Dresden) and Catch Factory (Leipzig) with [Aytaç Bahar](@/w/aytac-bahar.md), plus a mixed tag championship together with French wrestler Cory Zero.
In Poland, Joshua appeared occasionally in [PpW Ewenement](@/o/ppw.md) since 2023, both solo and as a tag team with Aytaç Bahar, since 2023.
