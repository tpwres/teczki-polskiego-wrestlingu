Iestyn Rees is a Welsh wrestler, best known in Poland for his work at [Kombat Pro Wrestling](@/o/kpw.md).

## Professional wrestling career

### Kombat Pro Wrestling (2023, 2026-)

Iestyn Rees made a one-off appearance at [Godzina Zero 2023](@/e/kpw/2023-08-18-kpw-godzina-zero-2023.md), where he defeated [David Oliwa](@/w/david-oliwa.md) and took part in a Championship Contract Battle Royal. Nearly three years later he returned to KPW to face [Zefir](@/w/zefir.md) at [Arena 33](@/e/kpw/2026-04-24-kpw-arena-33.md) for the vacated [KPW Championship](@/c/kpw-championship.md). The Welshman defeated the masked luchador, becoming the new KPW Champion.
