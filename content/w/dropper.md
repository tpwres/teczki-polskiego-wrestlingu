#### Transformation into Whiteblade (2026-)

After their loss at [PTW: Odrodzenie](@/e/ptw/2026-01-31-ptw-odrodzenie.md) Bump'n'Drop broke up. [Damian Lambert](@/w/damian-lambert.md) interfered in a post-match altercation between the duo. Driving Bumper off, he offered Dropper a lucha mask, which he rejected. Not accepting Dropper's rejection, Lambert laid him out with a German suplex and put the mask onto his face forcibly, carrying prone Dropper away from the arena.

At the [next show](@e/ptw/2026-02-21-ptw-world-cup.md) Lambert revealed himself as _the Prophet_. He prophesized the end of the world and promised to save everyone - just like he had saved a person so lost that even his name was Dropper. Now renamed Whiteblade, he made a full entrance wearing a lucha mask gifted by Lambert complete with a new, black and white gear.
