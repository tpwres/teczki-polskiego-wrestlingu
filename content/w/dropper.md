## Professional wrestling career

Dropper first appeared as a rookie at [All About the Moné](@/e/ptw/2025-07-27-ptw-all-about-the-mone.md), alongside rookies [Bumper](@/w/bumper.md) and [Boguś](@/w/bogus.md) in a Handicap Match against [Dawid "Puncher" Seńko](@/w/puncher.md) for the [PTW Championship](@/c/ptw-championship.md) and the BestYa Championship. He later made his official debut at [Halloweenowa Rozpierducha](@/e/ptw/2025-10-25-ptw-halloweenowa-rozpierducha.md)
