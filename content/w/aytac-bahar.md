Aytaç Bahar is a German wrestler, who fights primarily in Berlin-based GWF and Project Nova, but also all over the German scene, including two tag team championship runs with [Joshua Amaru](@/w/joshua-amaru.md) in ERW (Dresden) and Catch Factory (Leipzig).
In Poland, Aytaç occasionally appeared in [PpW Ewenement](@/o/ppw.md) since 2023, both solo and as a team with Joshua Amaru.
