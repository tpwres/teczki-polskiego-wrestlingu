## Professional wrestling career

### Kombat Pro Wrestling (2026-)

Sędzia Alan debuted on [Arena 31](@/e/kpw/2026-01-16-kpw-arena-31.md), where he shared duties with [Krystian Czekaj](@/w/krystian-czekaj.md). The two referees took turns officiating shows for a few month, until [Arena 34](@/e/kpw/2026-06-26-kpw-arena-34.md), which was Alan's first solo effort.
