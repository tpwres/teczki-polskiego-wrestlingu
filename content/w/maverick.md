Maverick is a Hungarian wrestler best known to Polish audiences from his appearances for [Prime Time Wrestling](@/o/ptw.md) and [PpW Ewenement Wrestling](@/o/ppw.md).

## Professional wrestling career

### Prime Time Wrestling (2024)

Maverick made his PTW debut at [PTW x Ryucon 3](@/e/ptw/2024-07-07-ptw-x-ryucon.md), winning a Fatal Four Way Match to kayfabe earn a PTW contract. This run was however short-lived - he made a single appearance for PTW at the [next show](@/e/ptw/2024-08-25-ptw-underground-22.md), losing to [Dawid "Puncher" Seńko](@/w/puncher.md) in a WWA Championship match. Following this show, Maverick did not take any more bookings in PTW.

### PpW Ewenement (2026-)

Maverick made his PpW debut as one of the unannounced participants in the Mystery X-Way Match at [Hardcore na Hucie](@/e/ppw/2026-04-25-ppw-hardcore-na-hucie.md). Subsequently, he continued to make appearances for PpW, mostly at promotion's excursions outside of Warsaw.
