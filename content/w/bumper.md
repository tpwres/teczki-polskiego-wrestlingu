## Professional wrestling career

### Prime Time Wrestling (2025-present)

#### Debut and early appearances

Bumper first appeared as a rookie at [All About the Moné](@/e/ptw/2025-07-27-ptw-all-about-the-mone.md) alongside rookies [Dropper](@/w/whiteblade.md) and [Boguś](@/w/bogus.md), in a Handicap Match against [Dawid "Puncher" Seńko](@/w/puncher.md) for the [PTW Championship](@/c/ptw-championship.md) and the BestYa Championship. He later made his official debut at [Halloweenowa Rozpierducha](@/e/ptw/2025-10-25-ptw-halloweenowa-rozpierducha.md). He portrayed a cocky persona of an overconfident rookie, which colided with his abilities to win against wrestlers such as [Sawicki](@/w/sawicki.md) and [Diana Strong](@/w/diana-strong.md). This led to him aligning himself with fellow rookie called Dropper to form "Bump & Drop" (also known as "Drop & Bump), Tag Team known for their lack of in-ring chemistry, which led to another series of defeats.

After their loss at [PTW: Odrodzenie](@/e/ptw/2026-01-31-ptw-odrodzenie.md) the duo broke up. [Damian Lambert](@/w/damian-lambert.md) interfered in a post-match altercation between the duo. Driving Bumper off, he offered Dropper a lucha mask, which he rejected. Not accepting Dropper's rejection, Lambert laid him out with a German suplex and put the mask onto his face forcibly, carrying prone Dropper away from the arena. At the [next show](@/e/ptw/2026-02-21-ptw-world-cup.md) Lambert revealed himself as _the Prophet_ and Dropper, Now renamed Whiteblade, became his follower, thus ensuring no reformation of Bump & Drop in the near future.

#### Transformation into Fabian

Over next weeks, Bumper's social media presented his transformation. After airing a _Confession_ video, airing his grievances about losing streak, he was approached by a new figure in PTW's universe - talent agent Dominique VaDique, leader of _Le VaDique Brand_. Instead of calling him Bumper, she used "Fabian", apparently his real name. At her behest, Bumper's transformation began. Over next weeks, he shared videos of attending fashion shows and theatre plays, gradually changing his look into more posh one and embracing "Fabian" identity. At some point he changed his Instagram handle to _@fabian_ptw_. Later on he cut his hair and declared that his ring name is now Fabian (pronounced by him "Fabią").
