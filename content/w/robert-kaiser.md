## Professional wrestling career

### Maniac Zone Wrestling (2014-2016; 2025)

Robert Kaiser made a few appearances in [Maniac Zone Wrestling](@/o/mzw.md) back in their early years, thanks to good relations between the Polish federation and the German scene, especially Dresden-based. He debuted at [MZW in Smolec](@/e/mzw/2014-06-21-mzw-untitled.md) show in June 2014. For the most part of his stint at MZW he served mostly as enhancement talent to fill the card with matches.
