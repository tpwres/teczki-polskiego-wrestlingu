Zaklęte Rewiry is a culture and entertainment centre in Wrocław. The venue usually hosts concerts of various musical acts, but has also held some of [PpW's](@/o/ppw.md) wrestling shows.

The name of the venue literally translates to _Enchanted Grounds_, which is the original Polish title of the film [_Hotel Pacific_][hotelowy-specyfik], based on the Henryk Worcell novel of the same name.
