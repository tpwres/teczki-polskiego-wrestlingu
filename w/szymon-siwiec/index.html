    Szymon Siwiec

    • Cagematch profile

    Szymon Siwiec is a Polish wrestler, who had a brief run with MZW between 2014 and 2017 as a singles competitor.

    Szymon is also the main character of a short documentary titled Amerykański Sen (American Dream), which focuses on his training to be a wrestler, overcoming stage fright and connecting with the audience.
