    PpW Ledwo Legalne II

    Ledwo Legalne II (Barely Legal 2) was PPW's 6th major event, held on Saturday, May 21, 2022. The name follows from the first Barely Legal event in 2021, itself both a reference to adult movies, and the fact that back in 2021 COVID-19 restrictions were lifted barely enough so that holding a wrestling event became legal again. It's notable for being the first PpW event not to be held at the 2Koła motorcycle club, and for introducing the PpW European Ultraviolent Championship. The event was held at Waldorffa 25, a dedicated event venue in Bemowo that would later become the main arena for PpW.
