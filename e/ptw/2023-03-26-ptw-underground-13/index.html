    PTW Underground 13

    The 13th monthly Underground show was held on Sunday March 26, 2023 at the PTW Performance Center in Chorzów.

    The event saw three foreign wrestlers, all from Hungary: the team of Dover and Icarus, known as Arrows of Hungary; and Hunyadi Tamas, who often teamed with Robert Star on the European independent circuit, mostly in Denmark.
