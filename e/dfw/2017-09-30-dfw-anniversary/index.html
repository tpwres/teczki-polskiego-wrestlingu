    DFW Anniversary

    Anniversary was an event that celebrated DFW's second anniversary, held on Saturday, September 30, 2017. The name was a bit of a stretch, as it was the second anniversary but when counting from DFW @ Kaszczorek. Which was DFW's second, not first show. Counting from the first show, the second anniversary was instead much closer to Crosswords City.

    The event was held in the sports hall belonging to Nicolaus Copernicus University, same one that hosted Tournament of Dreams and Recent Dreams.

    Featuring a battle royale match to select the next contender for Revage's DFW championship, and a street fight defense, this was probably DFW's biggest ever event. PpW's Cade Bruce contended in both a singles match and the battle royale.

    Recap and results

    • The #1 Contender match was ultimately meaningless, as Prince Victor never challenged Revage for the title. For both it was the last appearance in DFW, as by the time they and Direk already trained in PAW, and later that year joined MZW.
    • PJ Blake assumed ring announcer duty for this event.
