    MZW Charity Show 2015

    This was an event held by MZW in Głuchołazy. It was a charity show, with donations going to WOŚP, and coinciding with their 23rd annual Grand Finale event.

    In another corner of the country, DDW also held a charity event that same day, for the same cause.
