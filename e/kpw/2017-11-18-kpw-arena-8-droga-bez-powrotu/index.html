    KPW Arena 8: Droga Bez Powrotu

    Droga Bez Powrotu (Road of No Return) was the eight Arena show, and the first one (although second after Godzina Zero 2017) to be held at the Gdynia Sport Center.

    Card

    Toggle results

    • 1 Kaszub d. Boski Ostrowski and Peter Pannache and Gracjan Korpo KPW OldTown Championship
    • 2 Dawid Oliwa d. Kamil Aleksander
    • 3 Rosetti, Sawicki d. Mateusz Kowalski, Adam Bravo Tag Team Match
    • 4 Robert Star d. Greg(c) via DQ KPW OldTown Championship Match (KPW Championship #1 Contender Match for Robert)
    • No. Participants Details
    • 1 Boski Ostrowski vs Gracjan Korpo vs Kaszub vs Peter Pannache KPW OldTown Championship
    • 2 Dawid Oliwa vs Kamil Aleksander
    • 3 Mateusz Kowalski, Adam Bravo vs Rosetti, Sawicki Tag Team Match
    • 4 Greg(c) vs Robert Star KPW OldTown Championship Match (KPW Championship #1 Contender Match for Robert)

    Ring announcer: Arkadiusz Pawłowski
    Attendance: about 210

    Recap

    • In the main event, while Robert signals for his finisher, Greg pulls and slaps the ref to force a DQ, denying Robert the championship belt. However, he still wins his #1 Contender title shot.
