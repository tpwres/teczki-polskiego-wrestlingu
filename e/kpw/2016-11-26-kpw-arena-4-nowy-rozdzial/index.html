    KPW Arena 4: Nowy Rozdział

    Nowy Rozdział (New Chapter) was held in Gdańsk, in the gymnastics hall of CKZiU 1, a complex of various educational institutions at the high school or vocational school level. Much smaller in scope than the previous event, it had only one foreign guest, British wrestler Elliott Jordan.
